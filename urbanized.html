Urbanized Boutique

Urbanzied's Mission:

Urbanized Boutique is a small business located in Exton, Pa. Urbanized's goal is to bring the elegance of India's clothing to Exton while still delivering to customers out of state. Urbanized Boutique merchandise varies beyond just clothes; it displays both traditional Indian jewelry as well as modern fusion pieces. On top of pre-made dress's Urbanized allows clients to do custom orders on Indian dresses for Men,Women,and Kids.

Social Media Designer role:

For Urbanized Boutique, I got the creative freedom to design content for the brand to post on their social media. In addition to creating these post it was crucial to stay on top of trends and see what was successful in the social media world. The software I used for these creations would be canva.
