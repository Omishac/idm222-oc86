What is Lapstone & Hammer?

Laptsone & Hammer is a clothing and shoe store located in center city Philadelphia. Lapstone is a higher end store that displays brands like Golden Goose, Yeezy's, Vans, Nike, and even their own private label

Shopping Report role:

This shopping report was developed over 10 weeks for DSMR 103 which is Introduction to the Fashion industry. This course demonstrated the process of how to design, develop, and market a product in the industry, including implementing current vocabulary and foundation of knowledge about industry practices.
